History.

1913 - 1916

The Sun Pictures building was designed and constructed as a picture theatre that would seat up to 500 people in 1913.

Sun Pictures officially opened in Chinatown, Broome on December 9th 1916 with a silent movie, English Racing Drama ‘Kissing Cup’ with supporting ‘Bachelor Brown’ Comic.

2004

Sun Pictures was officially recognised by the Guinness World Records as being the "World's Oldest Open Air Cinema in Operation".

2016 -

Sun Pictures celebrates 100 years on 9th December in 2016 and opens as a commercial movie theatre well as a famous tourist destination in Broome until now.
